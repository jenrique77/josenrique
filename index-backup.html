Madonna Louise Ciccone (/tʃɪˈkoʊni/; born August 16, 1958) is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and businesswoman who is regarded as one of the most famous and successful performing artists in the world. A leading presence during the emergence of MTV in the 1980s, Madonna is known for pushing the boundaries of lyrical content in mainstream popular music and visual imagery in music videos. The artist has also frequently reinvented both her music and her image while maintaining autonomy within the recording industry. Her musical productions, which have generated both controversy and commercial success, have often by acclaimed by music critics. Referred to as the "Queen of Pop", Madonna is often cited as an influence by a variety of musicians.
